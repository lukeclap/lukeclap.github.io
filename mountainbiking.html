Mountain Biking

Exploring Mountainous Terrain

Mountain biking is a passion that takes me on adventures across the state of Maine. I often travel on my bike to enjoy the outdoors and access the hunting and fishing spots that I frequent during my adventures. Downhill mountain biking is also a hobby of mine, because it gives a rush of adrenaline and I can explore vast terain very fast. I have always had a need for speed and downhill mountain biking fulfills that need and enjoyment.
